Game Description

Gravity Fruits is a crash/combo mini game where fruits fall from the top of the arena into columns. A multiplier climbs in real time — cash out to bank your winnings or ride it until the game crashes.

  1. 1Player sets a bet via manual adjuster or slider
  2. 2Tap START — fruits fall, multiplier climbs
  3. 3Tap CASH OUT to bank bet × current multiplier
  4. 4Game can crash at any moment — pays nothing on crash
  5. 5START after result auto-resets and starts a new round
